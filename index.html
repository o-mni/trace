About LINKINT

BETA This tool is in active development — features may be incomplete or change without notice.

What is LINKINT?

LINKINT is a lightweight, browser-based OSINT workbench. It originated as a personal project to support basic Incident Response (IR) tasks — quickly triaging URLs and domains during an investigation without having to switch between multiple tools. It is intended for personal use only and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with any commercial product or organisation.

How it works

LINKINT does not perform any independent threat analysis. It acts as a client-side interface that queries publicly available third-party APIs and formats their responses in a structured, readable layout. All intelligence, data, and findings displayed originate entirely from the service providers listed below — LINKINT is simply a presentation layer built for fast IR triage.
